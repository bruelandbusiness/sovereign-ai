# Client Support Specialist - Sovereign AI

**Location:** Remote (US-based preferred)
**Type:** Full-time or Part-time (20-40 hrs/week)
**Reports to:** Founder / Head of Operations
**Compensation:** $55,000 - $75,000/year (full-time) | $28 - $38/hour (part-time)

---

## About Sovereign AI

Sovereign AI is a SaaS platform that provides AI-powered marketing and operations tools to home service businesses. We offer 16 integrated AI services -- from reputation management and SEO to automated ad campaigns and AI phone agents -- all managed through a single dashboard. Our clients are HVAC companies, plumbers, electricians, roofers, and other trades professionals who want to grow their businesses without becoming marketing experts.

We are a lean, fast-moving startup looking for our first dedicated support hire. You will be the primary point of contact for our clients and will have a direct impact on retention, satisfaction, and growth.

---

## The Role

As a Client Support Specialist, you will own the client experience from onboarding through ongoing support. You will handle inbound support tickets, conduct onboarding calls with new clients, troubleshoot service issues, and act as the bridge between our clients and the technical team.

This is not a script-reading call center job. You will need to understand our platform deeply, think critically about client problems, and communicate solutions clearly to non-technical business owners.

---

## Responsibilities

### Daily Operations
- Respond to client support tickets within SLA (first response within 2 hours during business hours)
- Triage and categorize incoming issues (billing, technical, configuration, feature request)
- Monitor service health dashboards and proactively flag anomalies
- Update client records and ticket notes in the admin panel

### Client Onboarding
- Conduct 30-minute onboarding calls with new clients within 48 hours of signup
- Walk clients through dashboard features, service configuration, and expectations
- Collect business information needed for service activation (Google Business Profile access, ad accounts, branding assets)
- Follow the onboarding SOP to ensure consistent activation across all 16 services

### Technical Support
- Troubleshoot common issues: AI agent misbehavior, review response quality, ad campaign performance, email deliverability
- Use internal tools (admin panel, Stripe, SendGrid, Twilio, Sentry) to diagnose problems
- Escalate complex technical issues to engineering with clear reproduction steps and context
- Test fixes and confirm resolution with clients

### Reporting & Communication
- Compile weekly support metrics (ticket volume, resolution time, common issues)
- Participate in weekly team syncs
- Document new issues and solutions in the support playbook
- Flag product improvement opportunities based on client feedback patterns
