# SERVICES.md — Product & Service Catalog

## SERVICE PHILOSOPHY

We don't sell "AI lead gen." We sell booked appointments and revenue growth. The AI is the engine — the client only cares about the output. Every service is measured by what it puts in the contractor's pocket, not what technology powers it.

Three rules for every service we offer:

1. The contractor should feel like they hired a full marketing department — not bought a software subscription
2. Results should be visible within 14 days — not "give it 90 days to work"
3. The service should get better every month — not flatline after setup
